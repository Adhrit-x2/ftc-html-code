Meet Team 21376

Mechanical Team

Dillon expand_more

Dillon has been with Oakwood's robotics program for three years now. He joined the middle school FTC team when he was in eighth grade, and has been a veteran since. He is also a passionate participant in Oakwood's spacecraft program, which has recently been selected as part of NASA's CubeSat Launch Initiative. Besides robotics, Dillon enjoys mountain biking and running cross country. This year, Dillon was only a sophomore but he took it upon himself to be the team leader. He was an integral part of this year's success, not only with his leadership skills but also by being one of the most hardworking mechanical builders.

Ethan expand_more

This is Ethan's second year in Oakwood's robotics program. He joined the middle school team last year and has continued on since. He is also very musically talented, especially when it comes to the piano, having also taken part in Oakwood's Jazz Ensemble class and performed. In this year's season, he played one of the most critical roles on the team, being one of our only CAD designers. He designed multiple parts for the robot and was vital for the team's achievement.

Adhrit expand_more

Adhrit has shown a remarkable level of dedication to his team this year. Having joined the middle school team last year, he continued on into his freshman year with commitment. This year, he was one of the team's three main CAD designers and collaborated with them to produce parts for the robot. Adhrit took countless hours out of his days to perfect his designs and create what was best for the team. Outside of robotics, Adhrit enjoys math, programming, soccer, badminton, and playing the drums.

Jake expand_more

Jake has been a part of Oakwood's FTC robotics teams for as long as they have existed. Back in 2020, he joined Oakwood's newly founded Gilded Gears, who have since renamed themselves to Build-A-Bot. He has persisted since then for four years and shown a great deal of mechanical knowledge and expertise. Jake, now a sophomore, is always reliable and puts forth a great deal of effort when it is necessary. At Oakwood, Jake also performed in the Guitar Performance Class and assisted with the theater performance.

Amanda expand_more

Amanda is a newcomer to the high school team this year. Despite this, she was a very helpful contributor to the team. She was a big part of this year's outreach efforts and assisted all around with various parts of the robot. A talented piano and clarinet player, she also performed in the Jazz Ensemble this year.

Alessandra expand_more

Alessandra joined the middle school team in the 2021 season and has stayed a veteran for 3 years now. Over time she has developed her skills in mechanical engineering and she has become a core part of the team. She is a natural team leader and has been the steadying factor that the teem needs. She is a very skilled soccer player and was even allowed to play on Oakwood's Boys Varsity team.

Thomas expand_more

Thomas is a graduating senior this year and will soon become a FIRST alumni. Though he was a newcomer this year, he has plenty of experience with robotics and is quite intelligent. He contributed a lot to the outreach program this year; he presented to his Boy Scouts troop about FIRST and robotics in general.

Software Team

Ryan expand_more

Ryan is a rookie player on the team this year, but he certainly has his fair share of software knowledge. This year, he took on the challenge of writing code for a Kalman Filter that could process a noisy image from a camera and get more refined details than given by the camera. With the circumstance of the programming team being so limited, he worked hard on the autonomous portion of the code.

Jeremy expand_more

Jeremy has been with Oakwood's robotics program for three years now, and he is very passionate about programming and math. He especially enjoys competition and was an important part of the team this year. With the programming team having only two people, he programmed most of the main drive code, including some of the autonomous program. At Oakwood, he is a participant in the Jazz Ensemble and enjoys performing music, especially piano. He has played piano for 10 years and learning the drums for the ensemble.

Business Team

Jonathan expand_more

Jonathan was an incoming freshman to both Oakwood and the robotics team. He rose to the occasion and was an incredible asset to have on the team. He worked alongside Gio to create the design prototypes for the robot in CAD. This organization was a vital step in the progression and advancement of the robot this year.

Giovanni expand_more

Giovanni is a new 9th grader at Oakwood and a rookie on the team. Alongside Jonathan, he worked hard and helped design the prototypes for the robot in CAD. He also played a big role in community outreach and helped all around the lab with the robot.

Aarush expand_more

Aarush is our other graduating senior and future FIRST alumni this year. Like Thomas, he was also a newcomer but had much prior knowledge and experience that heavily influenced our success this year. He helped build and design the robot and participated in a lot of outreach events.

Coaches

Michael Lyle expand_more

Our coach, Mr. Lyle, was the main team coach and encouraged us all to succeed and achieve our goals. He has been a volunteer at Oakwood since more than half a decade ago and has taken on the role of teaching and coaching robotics team and other STEM-related classes. He is a great coach and always guided us to find what we needed to solve all the problems we encountered.

Dawn Lyle expand_more

Mrs. Lyle teaches the elementary computer classes here at Oakwood and would often come in and assist our team when necessary. She was very encouraging and authoritative and could always keep the team motivated and working hard.

Mentors

Chris Foland expand_more

Once in a while, he'd come in and give us suggestions on what we could improve on and give his input on our designs.
