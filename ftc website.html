About Our Team

Our team consits of 12 people. (To learn more about us go to Our team page) We together are known as Boom Shaka Laka. We have been in the FTC enviorment for 2 years but with other teams that go to Oakwood we have been here for 4 years. Our team goals are to teach new people about FTC and about engineering in general. We also like to learn from our instructors and from whoever we can.

Our Outreach Efforts

In total we had 6 outreach projects in the year 2024. These projects are a Tour of CMS, Stem Presentations at JAMM, Social Media, this website, and a Libary Event. To learn more about this go to our Outreach page.

Team 21376's Achievements

During the Power Play year 2022-2023 our team got to States and almost to Worlds. The awards we got are: Inspire award and Innovate award. We also mentored our middle school team Build A Bot to getting themselves the second place Inspire Award.

Social Media

We have both Instagram and a Youtube accounts. We usually update what we are doing in the year on there. On our Youtube channel we upload tutorials and progress checks throughout the school year. These videos can be from helping with building lift kits or how to build an assembely in Onshape.
